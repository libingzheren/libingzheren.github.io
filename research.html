Emerging Storage Systems:

As the digital data volumn significantly increases, the requirement for data stored in the storage system also increases tremendously. Many emerging storage devices are coming out to satisfy the demand for high performance or large capacity. The projects focus on emerging storage devices such as Interlace Magnetic Recording, NVRAM, and DNA storage.
